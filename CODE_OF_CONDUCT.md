# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

The **stryker-netx** community pledges to make participation a welcoming and inclusive experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity. We commit to acting and interacting in ways that contribute to an open, healthy, and collaborative community.

## Adopted Standard

This project adopts the **Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct, Version 2.1**, in full and without modification.

The full text is published and maintained at:

- https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/
- (PDF/Markdown: https://github.com/EthicalSource/contributor_covenant)

By participating in this project — issues, pull requests, discussions, or any other interaction in project spaces — you agree to abide by that document.

## Scope

The Code applies in all project spaces (this repository, its issues/PRs/discussions, any official chat or mailing list) and when an individual is officially representing the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Reports of violations should be sent to the project maintainer:

- **Email:** Open a private security-style issue at https://github.com/pgm1980/stryker-netx/security/advisories or contact the maintainer directly via GitHub.

All complaints are reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Maintainers respect the privacy and security of the reporter.

Enforcement actions follow the **Community Impact Guidelines** defined in the Contributor Covenant 2.1 (Correction → Warning → Temporary Ban → Permanent Ban).
